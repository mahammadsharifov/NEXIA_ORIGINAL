Accounting

Each business needs reliable information and accurate bookkeeping as well as accounting. Your accounting information requirements will vary depending on the size and complexity of your organisation, but in all cases reliable information is the basic prerequisite for confidence in the value of an organisation for the investor and the public as well as for the organisation itself. Nexia professionals do more than deliver high quality accounting and business services.

Our accounting services cover the following:

  • Provision of daily accounting services as per the statutory standards and IFRS
  • Preparation and submission of the reports to the state authorities
  • Set-up and optimization of the accounting
  • Preparation of the tax reports
  • Audit of accounting and tax compliance
  • Inventory count of the Fixed Assets
